Currency

Cryptocurrencies have transformed the global financial landscape, offering decentralized, peer-topeer alternatives to traditional fiat currencies. Bitcoin, the first and most well-known cryptocurrency, sparked a revolution when it was introduced in 2009. Since then, thousands of cryptocurrencies have been developed, each with unique functionalities and purposes. This case study explores the basics of cryptocurrencies, reviews some of the most prominent ones, and provides an in-depth analysis of Bitcoin, focusing on its network, transactions, consensus mechanisms,
recent advancements
