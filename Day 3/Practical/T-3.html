Must-Visit Attractions

Kyoto Highlights:

  • Fushimi Inari Shrine - Famous for thousands of orange torii gates
  • Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion) - Zen temple covered in gold leaf
  • Arashiyama Bamboo Grove - Stunning bamboo forest

Reykjavik Adventures:

  1. Blue Lagoon geothermal spa
  2. Northern Lights viewing tours
  3. Golden Circle route exploration

Santorini Experiences:

  • Sunset watching in Oia
  • Wine tasting at local vineyards
  • Exploring ancient Akrotiri ruins

Machu Picchu Activities:

  • Hiking the Inca Trail
  • Guided archaeological tours
  • Visiting the Sacred Valley

Great Barrier Reef Activities:

  1. Snorkeling with tropical fish
  2. Scenic helicopter tours
  3. Glass-bottom boat excursions
