Why Visit Paris?

Paris offers a perfect blend of history, art, and modern culture. From world-class museums to charming cafes, from iconic landmarks to hidden gems, Paris has something for everyone.

Plan Your Trip

The best time to visit Paris is from June to August and September to October. The weather is pleasant, and the city comes alive with various events and festivals during these months.

Understanding Subscripts and Superscripts

In HTML, we use the following tags for subscripts and superscripts:

  • Superscript (<sup>): Used to display characters slightly above the normal line of text. Common in mathematical equations and ordinal numbers (1st, 2nd).
  • Subscript (<sub>): Used to display characters slightly below the normal line of text. Common in chemical formulas and mathematical notation.
